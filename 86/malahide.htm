HERZLICH WILKOMMEN IN IRLAND

On April 6, fifteen students and their teacher Andreas Bauer arrived from Prien-am-Chiemsee, Bavaria in Dubin Airport to spend 3 weeks learning English. They were hosted by parents of children attending Pobalscoillosa, Malahide and St. Marys Baldoyle. The day after their arrival

a reception was held in the library in Malahide Community School for the visitors from Germany and for their Irish partners and host parents. They were formally welcomed by Mrs. Bernie Malaone Chairman of the Board of Management, Mr. Pat Callan Acting Principal and by representatives from the Parents' Committee. They were also entertained to Irish dancing by Audrey Power, Kay McAdoo, Gemma Rinnce Maire NicAogain and even tried and succeeded in doing the Walls of Limerick!

Guided tours were arranged for them of Malahide Castle, Hugh Lane Gallery, National Art Gallery, National Museum and Dail Eireann where they were received by local T.D. G.V. Wright. The Host Parents in Malahide, Portmarnock and Raheny made them warmly welcome and very much at home, and indeed brought them on Clarke, Tracy Thornton and Angelina Ryan from Scoil sevaerl outings.

The kindness shown to the visitors was very much appreciate by them and this was confirmed in the telephone call from Prien immediately after their arrival back there. Their knowledge of English greatly improved during the time here and likely long lasting friendships between families in both countries will have been formed. The Irish pupils now look forward to the return visit on July 21st.
